>- only exists  during the time that your app  is open
>- has to have a name
>- `val`, `var`

>>	- number 
>>>	- `Byte` 8 ,`Short` 16, `Int` 32, `Long` 64, ); `val x: Short = 23`

>>	- boolean  `Boolean`
>>	- character `Char`
>>	- text `String` 
>>>	- `var name="adam"`, `name.lowercase()`
>>>	- konkatenacja `+`
